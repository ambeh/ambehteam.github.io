Overview

KASIT was established in year 2000, with the directives of His Majesty King Abdullah II in the development of IT in Jordan. All of the under-graduate programs in the faculty are accredited by the Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology) (ABET), which makes the faculty the first in Jordan to have all of its under-graduate programs gloabally accredited.
